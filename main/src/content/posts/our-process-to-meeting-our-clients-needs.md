At Global Music Solutions, we believe strong partnerships are built on trust, transparency, and proven results. That’s why we’ve designed an onboarding process that allows new clients to experience how we work before making a bigger commitment.

## Starting Small: The Trial Program
When a new client approaches us with a potential project, we don’t jump straight into a long-term contract. Instead, we offer a trial program that lasts anywhere from two weeks to a month. This initial phase gives both sides a chance to get to know each other and set clear expectations from the very beginning.

For a small, agreed-upon fee, we onboard the client into our User Acquisition (UA) system and start working on a smaller scale. During this period, our team focuses on delivering real, measurable results that align with the client’s goals.

## Understanding Needs, Proving Capabilities
The trial program isn’t just about showcasing our skills — it’s also about listening. We use this time to truly understand what the client wants and what success looks like for them. We test strategies, refine processes, and demonstrate that we can deliver quality work consistently.

## Building for the Long Term
At the end of the trial period, the results speak for themselves. If the client is happy — and they usually are — we move forward with a full project plan, backed by the trust and insights gained during the trial.

This approach helps clients feel confident in their decision to work with us, and helps us build partnerships that last.
